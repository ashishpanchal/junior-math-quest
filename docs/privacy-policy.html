🏆 Math Treasure Hunt — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Overview

Math Treasure Hunt is a children's educational math game designed for kids aged 5–8. We take your child's privacy seriously. This app is built with a privacy-first approach and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable data protection regulations.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Specifically, this app:

  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any personal information
  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertising
  • Does not make network requests or transmit data to any server
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not contain in-app purchases
  • Does not include chat, social, or messaging features
  • Does not link to external websites or third-party services

Local Data Storage

Game progress (levels completed, stars earned, coins, and settings) is stored locally on the device only using the device's built-in storage. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If the app is uninstalled, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Parental Controls

The app includes a parent-gated settings screen protected by an age-appropriate math question that only adults can solve. Parents can adjust game difficulty, timer settings, and sound/haptic preferences.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. There are no ad networks, analytics platforms, or social media integrations.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This app is designed for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. The app functions entirely offline with no data transmission capabilities.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date. Since we do not collect user data, we have no way to notify users directly of changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app's data practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email provided on our Google Play Store listing.
